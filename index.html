Welcome to Madicat's Cupcakes!

You're the new security guard? Ha! You won't last one night!
Sorry, that was...er...just a joke! Ha...ha...
Anyways, what I meant to say was:

"Welcome to Madicat's Cupcakes, the happiest cupcake shop on Earth! Here, kids can eat cupcakes with friends, have a sweet treat, or have a magical birthday party full of their favorite thing- sugar! At first glance, this just looks like any cupcake shop, right? Well, take a look inside and you'll meet our... special... staff!"

Well, there you go. There's your training.

Oh, one more thing- our "staff" is made of animatronic characters. You know, those robotic, animal-like things? Yea...I need to warn you about those. They...um...tend to get a bit...irritable...at night. So... be careful.

Meet the Staff!

  • Madicat
    • Your adorable feline friend!
    • The favorite of all the animatronics!
    • Friendly, and approachable! (Well, in the daytime, at least...)
    • Cute, fluffy, and hug-able! (Unless it's past 9:00 p.m.)
    • The main animatronic, acts as a leader to the others.
    • Kind to all, but fairly serious and mainly quiet.
    • When angry, exercise extreme caution.
    • Weakness: Jackrabbit.
    • Fun Fact: Called back 47 times for reported attacks (Only the extreme cases).
    • Lashes out at fellow animatronics when annoyed.
  • Jackrabbit
    • Closest to Madicat, favorite of Madicat.
    • Comedian of the staff, main entertainment.
    • Swift.
    • There are suspicions that at times he plans rebellions with Madicat. There is no proof, but many are said to have overheard their conversations.
    • Light, smooth fur
    • Closest (ally) to JoJo the Kangaroo.
  • Dee
    • Also known as, "The Cupcake Chickadee".
    • Based on a bird, particularly the Chickadee.
    • Famous for the song she sings as she works, "Sprinkles On Top!"
    • Has a slight glitch whenever someone says "tweet", making her shut down temporarily.
    • Bakes the cupcakes, and is the main singer of the animatronics.
    • When irritated, there are reports of her stabbing the guests with common utensils.
  • Natdog
    • Based upon a German Shephard
    • Closest (ally) to Madicat, her right-hand animatronic, even with the large differences.
    • Crazy, and manipulative when angry. (Tip: Avoid provoking anger.)
    • Youthful
    • Weakness and point of sanity: JoJo the Kangaroo.
    • Casualties are typically found with a large gash across their chest, along with mutilated throats.
  • JoJo the Kangaroo
    • Clever
    • Shares a mutual dislike with Madicat, frequent reports of them violently trying to destroy one another during breaks.
    • Closest to Natdog, favorite of Natdog.
    • Cute with fluffy fur, but can be very mischievous.
    • Casualties from her misbehavior are seen with deep claw marks on their chest and brutally mutilated throats.
  • Benda Bear
    • Smaller animatronic (1.5 foot tall- stuffed animal size).
    • Warning: Has retractable claws used to cut cupcakes for the younger children. It has been said that some days he has used these to savagely attack certain guests.
    • A misleadingly adorable panda bear.
    • Has a habit of climbing and traveling through air ducts. This can be very dangerous for anyone who attempts to fix or work in the ducts, so be very cautious.
    • Closest (ally) to Bryfox.
  • Bryfox
    • A prototype of the "Forest Friends" section of the restaurant.
    • Almost complete, but is missing his left ear and right arm.
    • Should be finished and released next year.
    • Currently resides in storage, but night shift workers have quit because of him, claiming that he roams the halls at night.
